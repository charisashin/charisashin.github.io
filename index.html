Nice to meet you!

I'm Charisa, pronounced like Karissa. Currently a sophomore at Brown University, I study Computer Science, with an emphasis on human-centered design. I spend a lot of my time pondering and exploring how code can shape and inform design, and vice versa. I also work as a UI/UX Specialist and Researcher for UCLA's HCI Lab.
