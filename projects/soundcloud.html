October 2019
Completed Project
Personal Project

Rapid Prototyping Branding UI/UX Design

BACKGROUND

Looking at SoundCloud in a different way

SoundCloud is a music-streaming platform created to expose users to both original and mainstream audio content. Its primary unique feature of universal uploading allows for any user to produce tracks, which has led to the mainstream emergence of several previously underground artists. I used to rely on SoundCloud extensively to listen to music (along with YouTube), but was always offput by its confusing visual design.

Since then, no major UI changes have been implemented, so I decided to give the redesign process a try in hopes of answering the question, "How can SoundCloud leverage its design to keep its primary user base?"

REFLECTIONS

Takeaways from the project

Completing this project cemented the idea that navigation, though seemingly simple, can make or break a design in whether or not it makes architectural sense. SoundCloud's needlessly complicated interface keeps users from returning to the interface and pushes them to look for a music app that provides more clarity. I believe it would be to SoundCloud's advantage if the company cleaned up some of their repetitive interface elements to emphasize the actual musical content that users and fans are searching for.
